# Livestream

## Gameplay

- Cost: 2
- Shapeshift into a random Idol Form.

The form-specific 0-cost card comes from `Prism Pendant`, not from `Livestream` itself.

## Art Direction

Cozy intimate streamer-at-home scene. Hologirl is medium-large in frame, seen in side profile at her desk, wearing a headset and facing a computer monitor. The computer and Hologirl are close to the camera. There is no concert crowd or fan background.

Hologirl remains blue/cyan with two readable ponytails. Gold/yellow is reserved for the monitor glow, projected UI accents, and tiny holographic effects.
