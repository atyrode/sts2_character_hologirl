# Signal Jab

## Role

Common attack that gives Hologirl a small audience bump without being just another `Strike`.

## Current Mechanics

- Cost: 1
- Rarity: Common
- Type: Attack
- Spend up to 2 `Fans`.
- Deal 6 damage, plus 3 for each `Fan` spent.
- If no `Fans` were spent, gain 1 `Fans`.
- Upgrade: +3 damage.

## Design Notes

This is the low-risk bridge between setup and payoff. With no audience, it seeds one `Fan`; with audience, it converts a small amount into efficient damage.
