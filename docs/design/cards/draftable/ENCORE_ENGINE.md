# Encore Engine

## Role

Uncommon setup power that connects passive audience growth with performance preservation.

## Current Mechanics

- Cost: 2
- Rarity: Uncommon
- Type: Power
- At the start of each turn, gain 1 `Fans`.
- Gain 2 `Singing`.
- Upgrade: +1 `Singing`.

## Design Notes

`Encore Engine` is the preservation setup: it keeps audience growth online and gives enough `Singing` to protect the growing Fan total while payoff cards come together.
