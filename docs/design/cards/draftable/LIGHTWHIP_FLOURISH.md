# Lightwhip Flourish

## Role

Uncommon multi-hit attack.

## Current Mechanics

- Cost: 2
- Rarity: Uncommon
- Type: Attack
- Spend up to 4 `Fans`.
- Deal 6 damage twice.
- For every 2 `Fans` spent, deal it 1 additional time.
- Upgrade: +2 damage per hit.

## Design Notes

This gives Hologirl a scalable multi-hit attack. It is less explosive than `Radiant Whip`, but it can turn a modest audience into a satisfying combo.
