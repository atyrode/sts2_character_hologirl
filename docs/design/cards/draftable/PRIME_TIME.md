# Prime Time

## Role

Rare defensive cashout for a built audience.

## Current Mechanics

- Cost: 2
- Rarity: Rare
- Type: Skill
- Spend all `Fans`.
- Gain `Block` equal to twice the `Fans` spent.
- For every 5 `Fans` spent, gain 1 energy, up to 3.
- Upgrade: gain 2 energy per 5 `Fans` spent instead, still capped at 3.

## Design Notes

`Prime Time` no longer grants `Livestream`. It is now the defensive counterpart to `Radiant Whip`: one cashes Fans into damage, the other into survival and a little tempo.
