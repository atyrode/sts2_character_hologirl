# Radiant Whip

## Role

Rare finisher attack that cashes out audience into burst damage.

## Current Mechanics

- Cost: 2
- Rarity: Rare
- Type: Attack
- Spend all `Fans`.
- Deal 14 damage, plus 2 for each `Fan` spent.
- Upgrade: +5 damage.

## Design Notes

This is the obvious damage payoff for protecting a large audience. It can still trigger `Livestream` big-hit reactions when the cashout is large enough.
