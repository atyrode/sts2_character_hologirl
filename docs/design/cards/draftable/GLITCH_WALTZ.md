# Glitch Waltz

## Role

Uncommon defensive rhythm card.

## Current Mechanics

- Cost: 1
- Rarity: Uncommon
- Type: Skill
- Gain 8 `Block`.
- Gain 1 `Singing`.
- If Hologirl has `Livestream`, draw 1 card.
- Upgrade: +3 `Block`.

## Design Notes

This card preserves audience through `Singing`, supplies real block, and becomes a smoother tempo card once the stream is live.
