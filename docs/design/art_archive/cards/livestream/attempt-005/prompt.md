# Prompt

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: Slay the Spire 2-inspired card portrait concept art for the Hologirl mod, landscape card-art composition.
Primary request: Create a new Livestream card art variant much closer to the iconic lofi hip hop radio girl framing: computer/monitor on the left side of the image, Hologirl on the right side, close intimate desk view, cropped around the middle of her torso rather than showing her full body. She is seated in side profile facing left toward the computer. The camera is close, like a cozy over-the-desk room scene, not a wide full-room view.
Subject: Hologirl, a blue/cyan hologram girl with two clearly readable long ponytails/twintails and a headset. She should occupy the right half of the frame, cropped around mid-torso, with hands near keyboard or desk. Her silhouette must remain simple and readable.
Scene/backdrop: Cozy streamer-at-home desk setup, simplified and intimate. The monitor/computer is on the left, angled consistently with her side profile. Desk edge and keyboard are visible. Room/background uses elegant desaturated pink and deep plum shapes. No crowd, no audience silhouettes, no light-stick crowd, no concert stage.
Style direction: Simple STS2-inspired card portrait, one bold readable skill image, flat chunky shapes, low color count, abrupt silhouettes, smooth matte shading, hard graphic edges, broad background shadow shapes, iconic readability at small card size. More graphic and restrained than anime illustration, not photorealistic, not glossy.
Color rules: Hologirl herself is blue/cyan hologram light. Yellow/gold is reserved only for monitor glow, projected UI accents, tiny holographic effects, and screen light. Use minimal glow and very sparse sparkles only where they imply holographic technology.
Composition constraints: Landscape image suitable for a 1000x760 card portrait crop. Computer/monitor on left, Hologirl on right, close crop at mid-torso, side profile facing left. Avoid full body, avoid distant framing, avoid showing her tiny in the room.
Negative prompt: detailed fantasy illustration, photorealism, anime screenshot, glossy mobile-game rendering, complex background, full room wide shot, full body character, character far away, crowd, fans in background, light-stick audience, concert stage, many colors, dense particle effects, readable text, card frame, UI logo, watermark.
