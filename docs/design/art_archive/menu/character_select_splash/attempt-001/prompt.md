# Prompt

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: Slay the Spire 2 character-select background splash art for the Hologirl mod, wide 2:1 composition suitable for a 2048x1024 static Godot TextureRect background.
Primary request: Create Hologirl's character selection splash art so she feels like an official Slay the Spire 2 character, not a mod. The image should match the vibe of STS2 character select backgrounds: bold hand-drawn fantasy shapes, matte texture, limited palette, large readable silhouette, dramatic but simple character staging, plenty of empty atmospheric space where UI panels can sit.
Composition: Wide landscape 2:1. Put most of the empty atmospheric background on the left and center. Place Hologirl as a large readable figure on the right third, facing slightly left toward the center, like she is being selected. Show her from roughly knees or waist up, not tiny. Her silhouette should be clear: blue/cyan hologram girl, two long ponytails/twintails, simple idol-like outfit shapes, calm confident performer stance. One hand loosely holds or trails a faint gold holographic light-whip that arcs subtly around her without dominating the frame.
Scene/backdrop: Dark fantasy stage-space rather than a modern room: desaturated deep plum, smoky magenta, and blue-black atmosphere, with abstract angular STS2 brush shapes. Add a few distant crowd/fan silhouettes as very subtle dark shapes low on the far left, with a few tiny gold light sticks, but keep them discreet and not cute or busy. Add a dim circular stage light or holographic projection floor shape under/behind Hologirl.
Style direction: Slay the Spire 2-inspired hand-drawn 2D fantasy splash, crisp graphic shapes, strong inked/painted edge definition, chunky silhouette, matte brush texture, low-to-medium detail, not highly rendered, not anime-clean, not photorealistic, not glossy. More polished than STS1 but still weird, handmade, and graphic.
Color rules: Hologirl herself is blue/cyan hologram light. Gold/yellow is only for things she creates or projects: light-whip, projected stage glow, sparse light sticks, tiny holographic particles. Background should mostly be desaturated plum/magenta/blue-black, not a one-note purple gradient.
Avoid: text, logo, UI, card frame, full anime illustration, glossy mobile game art, photorealism, detailed face rendering, detailed costume, busy concert crowd, modern computer setup, many colors, dense particles, perfect clean anime lineart, chibi proportions, extra characters.
