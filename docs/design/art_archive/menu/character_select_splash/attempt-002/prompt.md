# Prompt

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: Slay the Spire 2 character-select background splash art for the Hologirl mod, wide 2:1 composition suitable for a static Godot TextureRect background.
Primary request: Regenerate Hologirl's character selection splash art so she feels like an official Slay the Spire 2 character, not a mod. Keep the same core idea as the previous splash, but make her closer to the camera, more dramatic, and better framed for character select.
Composition: Wide landscape 2:1. Divide the screen into three vertical thirds: Hologirl should stand near the boundary between the middle third and the right third, not pushed to the far right edge. She should be closer to camera than before, roughly upper-thigh or waist-up framing, large enough to feel dramatic and selected. Leave atmospheric negative space on the left and some around her for UI, but do not make her tiny. Pose is calm and composed, not attacking and not preparing to attack.
Subject: Hologirl is a blue/cyan hologram girl with two long ponytails/twintails, a simple idol-like silhouette, and no headset. She is not gold. She should hold the handle of her weapon clearly: a golden sparkly holographic light-whip like the Strike card weapon. The whip should be displayed as an elegant glowing arc or coil beside/around her, with faint sparkles, but she is not swinging it. Her expression and posture should be confident, theatrical, and slightly mysterious.
Scene/backdrop: Dark fantasy stage-space rather than a modern room: desaturated deep plum, smoky magenta, and blue-black atmosphere, with abstract angular Slay the Spire 2 brush shapes. Add a dim circular stage light or holographic projection floor shape under/behind Hologirl. Crowd/fan silhouettes may be extremely subtle and low on the far left, with only a few tiny gold light sticks, but keep them discreet and not busy.
Style direction: Slay the Spire 2-inspired hand-drawn 2D fantasy splash, crisp graphic shapes, strong inked/painted edge definition, chunky silhouette, matte brush texture, low-to-medium detail, not highly rendered, not anime-clean, not photorealistic, not glossy. More polished than STS1 but still weird, handmade, and graphic. The character should fit next to official STS2 character select art.
Color rules: Hologirl herself is blue/cyan hologram light. Gold/yellow is only for things she creates or projects: light-whip, projected stage glow, sparse light sticks, tiny holographic particles. Background should mostly be desaturated plum/magenta/blue-black, not a one-note purple gradient.
Avoid: text, logo, UI, card frame, headset, headphones, microphone headset, computer setup, full anime illustration, glossy mobile game art, photorealism, detailed face rendering, detailed costume, busy concert crowd, many colors, dense particles, perfect clean anime lineart, chibi proportions, extra characters, action attack pose, whip strike pose.
