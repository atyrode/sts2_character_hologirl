# Prompt

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: Slay the Spire 2 character-select background splash art for the Hologirl mod, wide 2:1 composition for preview only.
Primary request: Create a third character-select splash attempt for Hologirl. Keep the improved composition idea from attempt 002, but return to the less realistic, more graphic, handmade drawing style of attempt 001 and official Slay the Spire 2 character select art. This must feel like it belongs in Slay the Spire 2, not like polished fantasy splash art.
Style baseline: Slay the Spire 2-inspired hand-drawn 2D fantasy art. Bigger and more animated-feeling than STS1, but still simple, odd, and handmade. Use chunky readable silhouettes, crisp hard-edged painted shapes, visible brush texture, flat/matte color blocks, low-to-medium detail, limited rendering, and stylized anatomy. Avoid smooth realism, photoreal lighting, realistic face shading, glossy mobile-game polish, Magic-the-Gathering detail, and clean anime rendering. Prioritize graphic readability over beauty.
Composition: Wide landscape 2:1. Hologirl should stand near the vertical boundary between the middle third and the right third, not at the far right edge. She is closer than attempt 001 but fully in frame: head, both ponytails, torso, weapon handle, and enough upper body must be visible with clear padding above the head and ponytails. Use upper-thigh or waist-up framing, but do not crop the top of her head. Leave atmospheric negative space on the left and center for UI.
Subject: Hologirl is a blue/cyan hologram girl with two long ponytails/twintails, simple idol-like silhouette, no headset, no headphones, no microphone. She is not gold. Her face is simple and stylized, with minimal rendering. She calmly holds the handle of her weapon: a golden sparkly holographic light-whip like the Strike card weapon. The whip is displayed as an elegant glowing arc or loose coil beside/around her, not being swung. She should look theatrical and slightly mysterious, but not attacking.
Scene/backdrop: Dark fantasy stage-space with desaturated deep plum, smoky magenta, blue-black, and rough angular brush shapes. Add a simple dim circular stage/projection glow behind or under Hologirl. A few very subtle fan/crowd silhouettes may sit low on the far left with tiny gold light sticks, but keep them sparse and abstract.
Color rules: Hologirl herself is blue/cyan hologram light. Gold/yellow is only for projected things she creates: light-whip, sparse sparkles, stage glow, tiny light sticks. Background uses muted plum/magenta/blue-black, with clear graphic planes instead of a smooth gradient.
Avoid: text, logo, UI, card frame, headset, headphones, microphone, computer setup, cropped head, cropped ponytail tops, full anime illustration, realistic rendering, glossy lighting, high-detail face, high-detail costume, busy crowd, dense particles, many colors, chibi proportions, extra characters, attacking pose, whip strike pose.
