# Prompt

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: clean animation rig sheet for a Slay the Spire 2 style character-select puppet prototype.
Primary request: Create a cleaner second-pass layered puppet art sheet for Hologirl. This is not a scene; it is a technical rigging sheet meant to be split into separate transparent PNG layers for Godot.

Canvas and background: perfectly uniform flat #00ff00 chroma-key background, no shadows, no floor plane, no texture, no gradient, no text, no labels. Keep every part separated with generous empty green padding so automated connected-component splitting can isolate pieces.

Character identity: Hologirl is an expressionless cyan-blue hologram girl, simple idol/combat outfit, two large twin ponytails, glowing rectangular eyes only, no mouth, no detailed face. She uses a golden projected light-whip. Body is blue/cyan hologram material. Gold is reserved only for the whip, handle accents, and tiny projected sparkle pieces.

Rig sheet layout: Arrange parts in a clean readable grid, each part fully visible and not touching any other part. Include these separate complete parts: head with front hair, left ponytail, right ponytail, torso/dress block, skirt/hip piece, left upper arm, left forearm and hand, right upper arm, right forearm and hand holding the whip handle, left thigh/leg, right thigh/leg, left boot/foot, right boot/foot, one long separate golden whip curve, 6 to 10 small separate golden sparkle pieces. Do not connect the whip to the hand; place the long whip curve as its own isolated part.

Animation readiness: Each limb part must include rounded hidden overlap at its joint end, like a 2D puppet part designed to rotate behind another piece without exposing gaps. Pivots should be visually obvious from the shape: rounded shoulder caps, elbow overlap ends, neck overlap, hip overlap, ponytail root caps. Avoid hard cropped joint cuts. Avoid parts that interpenetrate or fuse together.

Style: Slay the Spire 2 compatible handmade fantasy UI art. Matte flat color masses, chunky graphic silhouette, rough crayon/pastel edge texture, simple cel-shaded shadow blocks, strong readable outlines/edges, intentionally handmade and slightly odd. Lower-detail than anime key art. Not photorealistic, not glossy, not highly rendered, not Magic the Gathering realism, not clean anime vector art.

Quality constraints: Preserve symmetry of the two ponytails. Make each part large enough to inspect. Keep color count restrained: blue/cyan body with darker blue shadow blocks and sparse light highlights; golden whip/sparkles with darker gold shadow blocks. No green anywhere in the subject.
