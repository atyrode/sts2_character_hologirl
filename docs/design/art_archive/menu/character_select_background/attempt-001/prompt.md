# Prompt

Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: Slay the Spire 2 character-select background plate, 1774x887 wide scene, no foreground character.
Primary request: Create a background for Hologirl's character-select scene that can sit behind a separate transparent character cutout.
Scene/backdrop: a dark crystalline idol-stage arena inside a spire-like fantasy chamber, with a circular stage platform slightly left of center, faint crowd silhouettes far in the lower-left holding tiny golden light sticks, tall angular crystal panels and banner-like shapes in the back, no readable text, no character, no weapon.
Style direction: Slay the Spire 2 inspired hand-drawn fantasy UI art, flat pastel/crayon texture, crisp graphic shapes, bold readable silhouettes, limited palette, dark desaturated purple-pink and blue-black environment with small warm gold accents. Matte and handmade, not glossy, not photorealistic, not anime-clean, not high-detail Magic-style realism.
Composition: keep the center-right area readable and darker so a bright blue hologram character can layer on top; keep the bottom and edges visually grounded; leave no important subject near the extreme edge.
Rendering constraints: broad flat planes, visible crayon/pastel grain, selective hard shadow blocks, minimal detail noise, no foreground character, no face, no body, no whip.
